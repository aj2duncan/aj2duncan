Research

Current Research

I am currently working at both the SRUC Epidemiology Research Unit where I mainly model disease spread through herds of cattle and at the River and Lochs Institute where I do some programming and statistical modelling. I also do a little bit of website design and work a lot with the statistical software R.

Websites and Software

  • Strandings.org is the website of the marine stranding scheme scotland and something I have been working with for a few years. This website has details of strandings (once they have been confirmed and/or post mortemed) and they can be mapped or basic statistical charts shown.
  • The Online Epidemiology resource centre which I built as part of my work with the Epi Unit. It hosts a number of online models and calculators all built with shiny, including a Two-stage Sample Size Calculator to help design surveys to estimate Herd Level Prevalence when the individual test is imperfect.
  • My own shiny server to use for any apps I develop for teaching or just for my own interest.
